👋 Set up your season in 3 steps
1
Add your meets — scroll down to "Add a Meet" and enter your key competitions. A-meets get a full taper; B-meets a partial one.
2
Set your season template — go to the More tab and find "Season Template". Enter your season dates and set each weekday to Swim / Dryland / Double / Rest, then tap Apply to Season. The calendar fills automatically.
3
Tap any day to override — once the template is applied, tap any calendar day to change that day's session type individually.
